VRTEX Economy Commands

🏦 Economy

  • vebalance – Check wallet and bank balance
  • vedeposit [amount] – Deposit money into your bank
  • vewithdraw [amount] – Withdraw money from your bank
  • vetransfer [@user] [amount] – Send money to another user
  • veleaderboard – View the richest users
  • veprofile [@user] – View your or another profile

💼 Work & Jobs

  • vework – Work to earn coins
  • veapplyjob [name] – Apply for a job
  • vequitjob – Leave current job
  • vejobs – List all jobs
  • vepromote – Get promoted automatically when eligible

🏠 Business

  • vebusiness buy [name] – Purchase a business
  • vebusiness upgrade [name] – Upgrade a business
  • vebusiness list – View all available businesses
  • vebusiness claim – Collect profits

💎 Marketplace

  • veinventory – Check items
  • veuse [item] – Use an item
  • vesell [item] – Sell an item
  • vebuy [item] – Buy an item
  • vemarket list – View listings

🧭 Adventure & Quests

  • veadventure – Explore and find rewards
  • vequests – Check current quests
  • veachievements – Show achievements
